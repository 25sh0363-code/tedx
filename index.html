Event Theme 2025

Maya: Illusions in Daily Life

Breaking through the veils of perception

Maya reflects the subtle yet powerful illusions that shape how we see and understand the world. In our everyday lives, perception often outweighs truth, guiding the way we think, act, and respond to the world around us.

Identity & Self

Questions about whether the self is fixed or shaped by expectations around us.

Time & Efficiency

Our relationship with time, making us feel rushed in an age designed for efficiency.

Connection & Distance

The promise of being constantly linked may hide a sense of distance.

By exploring the various notions presented to us, we start the journey to unearth a reality beyond the clutches of Maya and take control of our paradigm.
