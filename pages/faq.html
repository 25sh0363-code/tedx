Everything you need to know about TEDx Silver Oaks

TEDx Silver Oaks will take place on 20th December 2025, from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM. Get ready for a full day of inspiring talks, performances, and networking!

The event will be held at Silver Oaks International School, Bachupally, Hyderabad. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities for an unforgettable TEDx experience.

Mobile phones are allowed for emergency purposes, but we kindly request that all devices be kept on silent mode during talks to maintain an engaging atmosphere for all attendees.

You can register right here on our website! Simply visit our Registration Page, fill out the form, complete the payment, and upload your payment proof. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is verified.

Business Formals – Dress professionally for the event. This is a formal occasion, so please wear appropriate business attire.

To ensure everyone has a great experience, please:

  • Arrive on time – doors open at 9:00 AM
  • Keep mobile phones on silent during talks
  • Be respectful and attentive during presentations

  • Participate actively in Q&A and networking sessions
  • Follow the event schedule and announcements
