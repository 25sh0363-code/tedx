Meet Our Speakers

Dr. Virinchi Sharma

Psychiatrist

Dr. Virinchi Sharma is a distinguished Psychiatrist, Addiction Psychiatrist, and Geriatric Psychiatrist with over a decade of experience in mental health care. He was the state topper in Psychiatry in 2015. He completed his MBBS from PES Institute of Medical Science & Research and his MD in Psychiatry from SVS Medical College, Mahabubnagar. Currently practicing at Manasa Nursing Home and Arete Hospitals in Hyderabad, Dr. Virinchi is widely recognized for his empathetic approach and deep understanding of human behaviour. As a TEDx speaker, media panellist, and recipient of the Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Memorial Excellence Award in 2020, he has been a strong voice in promoting mental health awareness and breaking the stigma surrounding it. His work reflects compassion, clarity, and commitment, empowering individuals to heal, grow, and rediscover balance in their lives.

Dr. Mudrika Khandelwal

Author | Associate Professor

Dr. Mudrika Khandelwal is an Associate Professor in the Department of Materials Science and Metallurgical Engineering, known for her pioneering work at the intersection of nanoscience and biotechnology. A PhD graduate from Lucy Cavendish College, University of Cambridge, and an alumna of IIT Bombay, she has dedicated her career to exploring cellulose composites and biomedical materials. Beyond her academic excellence, Dr. Mudrika is driven by a deep curiosity and a genuine desire to make science more purposeful and accessible. Drawing on her fascination with the natural world, Dr. Mudrika co-authored the book Demystifying the Nature, delving into the wonders of the natural world. Her journey is a combination of intellect, empathy, and innovation. She is constantly trying to inspire young minds to think critically and contribute meaningfully to a sustainable future.

Uma Sudhir

Executive Editor, NDTV

Uma Sudhir is one of the most recognised voices in Indian journalism. She is a reporter who covers stories with absolute truth and clarity. As the Executive Editor of NDTV's South India bureau, she has spent years documenting everything from environmental crises to human-impact stories. Her academic journey is just as impressive: a mix of science, governance, and storytelling. She has completed her B.Sc. from Hindu College at the University of Delhi, a Post Graduate Diploma in Science and Social Journalism from the Times Research Foundation, and a Master's in Public Administration from Panjab University. Today, she stands as a journalist who has shaped public awareness, inspired young reporters, and consistently reminded the country why honest reporting still matters.

Mr. Anuj Joshi

Learning & Development Head

Born in the serene hills of Shillong, Mr. Joshi carries with him a belief that has shaped both his life and career, that learning involves knowledge acquisition as well as growing, staying curious, and creating real impact. And throughout his professional journey, he has lived that philosophy wholeheartedly. He started on the shop floor at Tata Motors, and over the last 20+ years, he's moved through some of India's biggest industries: automobiles, telecom, IT, and now pharmaceuticals. As a General Manager at Aurobindo Pharmacy, he has played a pivotal role in shaping strategies that bridge pharma and technology.

He was part of the team behind the Money Heist Netflix promo and has taken on roles as director, writer, animator, actor and creative director. Abhinav Krishna

Student at Silver Oaks International School, Bachupally

Meet Abhinav Krishna, a Class 12 science student who embodies determination, critical thinking, and the kind of patience that turns challenges into opportunities. Known for his ability to adapt quickly and listen deeply, he approaches every obstacle with the intensity of a raging bull, focused and unwavering in the pursuit of success. An aspiring Robotics Engineer, he is passionate about building machines that solve real-world problems and create a pivotal impact. His curiosity fuels his creativity, and his discipline fuels his journey. Beyond his technical pursuits, Abhinav is a spirited debater who thrives in high-pressure moments, never backing down when the odds are stacked against him. A true believer in hard work, he aims to be a global citizen, one bold step at a time. Away from the world of engineering and debating, you'll find him with the same determined spirit, tackling a football across the field or exploring his interests.

Tanvi Jalnapurkar

Student at Silver Oaks International School, Bachupally

Meet Tanvi Jalnapurkar, a Class 11 humanities student at Silver Oaks International School, Bachupally with a proclivity for public speaking and creative writing. She has anchored major school events, served as Head Girl, won essay writing competitions, published her own poetry book, and even qualified for the youth national level parliament. But beyond the achievements is the person she is becoming. She dabbles in various artistic endeavours, ranging from sketching to creative writing, to process the world around her. As an avid debater, she finds strength in the process and pursues it with the same steady determination that defines all her goals. She remains undeterred and committed to giving her best.
