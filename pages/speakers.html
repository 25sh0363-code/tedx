Meet Our Speakers

Speaker announcements coming soon

Dr. Virinchi Sharma

Psychiatrist | Mental Health Advocate

Dr. Virinchi Sharma is a distinguished Psychiatrist known for his clarity, empathy, and commitment to mental health. With over a decade of clinical experience at Manasa Nursing Home and Arete Hospitals, he combines strong academic grounding, MBBS, and MD (Psychiatry) from NTR University of Health Sciences, with award-winning expertise, including the IPS State-Level Quiz First Prize, Torrents Young Scholar Award, and a state 3rd rank in Psychiatry.

A TEDx speaker, media panelist, and recipient of the Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Memorial Excellence Award, he also leads Fighting Blues, driving mental health awareness with professionalism and impact.

Dr. Mudrika Khandelwal

Professor, IIT Hyderabad | Materials Science Researcher

Dr. Mudrika Khandelwal is a full-time professor at IIT Hyderabad in the Department of Materials Science and Metallurgical Engineering. A Ph.D. graduate from the University of Cambridge and an alumna of IIT Bombay, she specializes in cellulose, composites, and bio-inspired materials as part of the PI Cellulose and Composites Group. Her work sits at the intersection of nanoscience and biotechnology, driven by a commitment to purposeful research, accessibility in science, and inspiring young minds to think critically for a sustainable future.

Mr. Anuj Joshi

General Manager, Aurobindo Pharmacy | Strategy & Operations Leader

Mr. Anuj Joshi is a seasoned professional with over two decades of experience across the automotive, telecom, IT, and pharmaceutical sectors. Starting at Tata Motors, he built a strong technical and managerial foundation that shaped his leadership journey. A certified global trainer with advanced studies in management and communication, he specialises in strategy, operations, and process design. In his current role as General Manager at Aurobindo Pharmacy, he drives digital integration, automation, and smart learning initiatives, strengthening efficiency and modernising pharma operations.

Uma Sudhir

Executive Editor, NDTV | Journalist

Uma Sudhir is a widely respected journalist known for her sharp, honest, and impactful reporting. As Executive Editor of NDTV's South India bureau, she has covered environmental issues, public policy, and human-interest stories with depth and credibility. She holds a B.Sc. from Hindu College, a PG Diploma in Science and Social Journalism, and a Master's in Public Administration. With strong academic grounding and decades of field reporting, she continues to set high benchmarks in journalism and remains an influential voice in India's public narrative.

Yuvrajaago

Multidisciplinary Filmmaker | Co-Founder, Production House

Yuvrajaago is a multidisciplinary filmmaker from Jaipur, known for his fresh blend of cinema, music, and modern visual storytelling. In just two years, he has created 20+ music-led projects and contributed to India's Oscar entry Chello Show. His work includes collaborations with Ed Sheeran, Savan Kotecha, Jaaved Jaaferi, Anil Kapoor, Shruti Haasan, and even the Money Heist Netflix promo. Skilled across direction, writing, animation, and editing, his films have screened at ALP Film Festival and in theatres across Mumbai and Kerala. As co-founder of his own production house, he continues to build bold, inventive visual narratives.
