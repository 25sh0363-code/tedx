Head Organizer

Dhanyatha Dosapati

A dedicated twelfth grade commerce student at Silver Oaks International School and the visionary lead organizer of this TEDx event. Known for her discipline, creative flair and her extraordinary leadership skills, Dhanyatha truly excels at her craft. During her pastime, she is often found immersing herself in fictional literature or dancing to her heart's content. Beyond her organizational pursuits, she is passionate about writing as she publishes her articles in the school magazine and she is an established young author in the Scholastic Writer's Academy. Dhanyatha's inclination towards public speaking sparked her to provide a platform for others via TEDx. With her unwavering commitment and her ability to motivate and lead her peers, Dhanyatha remains steadfast in her vision to make TEDx Silver Oaks Intl School Bachupally a tremendous success.

Mrs. Nisha Mathew serves as the Senior Secondary Coordinator at Silver Oaks and is also a certified career counselor. Her enduring passion for Chemistry, particularly Organic Chemistry, has guided and inspired generations of students in grades 11 and 12. She is admired for her clarity of thought, her steady patience, and her ability to turn complex ideas into meaningful, accessible learning. This makes her both approachable and dependable. As a co-organizer of TEDx, she brings a distinct leadership style marked by collaboration, creativity, and purpose. She encourages fresh ideas, nurtures a culture of continuous improvement. Her leadership isn't just about coordinating teams; it's about inspiring them to do their best work.
